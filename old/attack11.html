Explanations for each field of email editor




In the following page, you are going to have an email editor. Please keep the fields and descriptions in mind. Also you can refer to the paper version of the explanations.

Field Description
Your name Your actual first and last name
Email type ‘authentic’ or ‘phishing’ based on your assigned condition
Desired email address You can craft any email address but are not allowed to use UNCC account
Receiver’s email Receiver’s email address given by the researcher
Sent time Desired time of day when you expect this email to be sent
Sent day Desired day when you expect this email to be sent
Email subject Subject line for this email (if any)
Email body - Length of the body should be more than 50 words
- You can insert any image and link or add the attachment
- You can format the email text by setting font style, size, and color, etc
- You can search for related information on the Internet
Key sentence After crafting the email, copy and paste one sentence that is the key to its fakeness
