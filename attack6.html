Field Description
Your name Enter Your actual first and last name
Email type Enter ‘authentic’ or ‘phishing’ based on your assigned condition
Your email address Enter email address but are not allowed to use UNCC account
Receiver’s email Enter receiver’s email address given by the researcher
Sent time Enter desired time of day when you expect this email to be sent
Sent day Enter desired day when you expect this email to be sent
