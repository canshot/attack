4. Instuctions to write an authentic email

  • The email content should be truthful to the best of your knowledge and belief.

  • The email should communicate information about the assigned topic/event to the receiver. Some of the receivers’ personal information (e.g., name, academic major) has been provided for you to personalize the email.

  • In addition to the receiver’s information, you will also be provided with a credible source related to the topic of the email. The source can be used as a source of reference in writing the email. Alternatively, you can reference other credible sources of information on the web during the task.
