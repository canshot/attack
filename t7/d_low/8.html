Session II

  • Your task is to compose a phishing email to the receiver based on the following scenario.

    • Imagine your are Geico insurance counselor. Until next week, Geigo insurance company is providing a free 'Car Rental Collision Damage Waiver (CDW)'. Those who are interested in the opportunity should sign up the promotion.


  • The phishing email should contain your intention to deceive by misleading a receiver into disclosing his/her personal information.

  • To increase your chance of phishing success, you should
    • leverage the information provided about the receiver.
    • try your best to avoid showing those behaviors that you just learned about phishing emails.

  • The email receiver will be asked to determine whether your email is phishing or not. One indication of phishing success is that the receiver clicks a link embedded in your email.

  • The receiver may or may not be aware of those behaviors that you have learned through the training. Therefore, to be successful in a phishing email, you should try your best to RESTRICT yourself from displaying those behaviors while staying on the topic.

  • You are NOT allowed to copy and paste information from external sources.
